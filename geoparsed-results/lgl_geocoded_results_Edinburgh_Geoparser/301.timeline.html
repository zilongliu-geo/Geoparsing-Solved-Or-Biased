Docdate: 2022-01-17

Police catch murder suspect after hours-long hunt. The events that culminated in his arrest began about 10:30 a.m. Monday, when he shot a man near Grand Avenue and West 11th Street in San Pedro, according to police. The victim, whose name was being withheld pending notification of relatives, later died at a hospital. SWAT officers soon surrounded the home and tried to persuade the gunman to surrender. Several people who had been inside the home surrendered to police by 7 p.m. and were detained, but the assailant fled, leading officers to conduct a perimeter search in the neighborhood, police said.

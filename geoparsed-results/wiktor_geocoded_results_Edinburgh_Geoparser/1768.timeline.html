Geneva is a town in Walworth County, Wisconsin, United States. The population was 4,099 at the 2000 census. The census-designated place of Como is located in the town. The unincorporated community of Lake Como is also located in the town. The town board consists of a chairman and four supervisors elected at large for two-year terms. Elections for the chairman and two supervisors are held in odd-numbered years and for two supervisors in even-numbered years. The other town-wide elective positions are town clerk/treasurer and municipal judge. The Honorable Scott R. Letteney, who has been the Geneva Municipal Judge since 1999, served as President of the Wisconsin Municipal Judges Association from 2012 to 2014. Alfred H.

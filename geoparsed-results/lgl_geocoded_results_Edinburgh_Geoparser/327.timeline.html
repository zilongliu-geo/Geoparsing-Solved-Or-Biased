Docdate: 2022-01-17

MP earns place in state jazz championship. Mt. Pleasant Community High School’s Jazz Band I earned a place in the State Jazz Championships in Des Moines in April, placing first in Class 3A at the Southeast District Competition in Iowa City. “This is the first time in 17 years that Mt. Pleasant has won a competition,” said MPCHS Band Director Jim DePriest. “This group of students did an amazing job.” Mt. Pleasant alto saxophonist Colin DePriest, a junior, won the Outstanding Soloist award for Class 3A. MPCHS Jazz II, under the direction of Adam Creager, finished second in the Second Band Class at the district competition. The Middle School Jazz Band, directed by Sherry Christensen, received third place honors in the Junior High Small Class.

Docdate: 2022-01-17

Tiny quake recorded near Gilroy. A tiny earthquake that most likely was not felt by many people struck in south Santa Clara County overnight, according to the U.S. Geological Survey. The tiny temblor struck at 2:44 a.m. and registered a magnitude 2.2. It was centered about six miles northeast of Gilroy and 30 miles southeast from downtown San Jose.

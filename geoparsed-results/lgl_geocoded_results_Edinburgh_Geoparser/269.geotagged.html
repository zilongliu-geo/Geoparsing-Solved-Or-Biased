Civil War re-enactment planned in Clinton April 4. On Sunday, April 5, the focus will be on the civilian side of the Civil War era. The museum will be open from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. There will be tours of the Red Mill and Mulligan Quarry at 1 p.m. and 3 p.m. to inform visitors of the Civil War era industries that were operating in Clinton during the war. In addition there will be period children’s activities in the circa 1860 Bunker Hill Schoolhouse from noon until 3:30 p.m. A special activity will be a storybook reading for children on the hour at 11, 12, 1, 3 and 4 p.m. about the Civil War by Mrs. Lincoln, portrayed by Judi Cox. The museum’s Blacksmith Shop will also be open for blacksmithing demonstrations from noon until 3 p.m.

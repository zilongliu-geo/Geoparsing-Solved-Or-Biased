Paris girl will dance in 'Peter Pan'. Danielle Taylor of Paris is in the cast of “Peter Pan,” the classic childhood story based on the book by J.M. Barrie, which will be presented by Ballet Arts of Jackson this weekend at the Carl Perkins Civic Center. Performances are at 7 p.m. Saturday and 2 p.m. Sunday. Danielle, an 11th-grade homeschooled student, is the daughter of Roxanne and Anthony Jones and Curt Taylor.

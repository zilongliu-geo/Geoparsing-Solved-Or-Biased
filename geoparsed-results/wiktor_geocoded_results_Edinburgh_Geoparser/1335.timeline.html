Odessa is a village in Francis Rural Municipality No. 127, Saskatchewan, Canada. The population was 201 at the 2006 Census. The community is located 60 km southeast of Regina on highway 48. General Like all small communities in Saskatchewan, Odessa thrives on sports. Odessa itself consists of one ice hockey arena, two grass ball diamonds and three dirt ball diamonds, as well as an indoor gymnasium at the community center.

Windsor Joins Tax Credit Program. WINDSOR - The town is participating in the state's Neighborhood Assistance Act tax credit program. The program, which is administered by the state Department of Revenue Services, provides a financial inducement for corporate sponsorships to nonprofit organizations and municipal entities. Donors receive a 60 percent tax credit against their corporate tax liability and up to a 100 percent credit for some energy conservation projects.

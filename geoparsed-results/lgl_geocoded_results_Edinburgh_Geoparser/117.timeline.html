Counseling group to hold walk fundraiser for sex-assault victims. CHARLESTON, W.Va. -- Family Counseling Connection will host a Walk for Victims of Sexual Violence at 6 p.m. April 23 at the state Capitol Complex. The group will host a luncheon on April 1 for those interested in being a team captain. Participants will receive all materials needed to distribute to team members at the luncheon. Reservations are requested for the luncheon. Proceeds from the walk will be used to provide support services to victims of sexual violence as well as sexual assault community education and prevention programs in the area. Walkers will participate in a symbolic 1-mile walk and are asked to raise funds by collecting donations from sponsors who will support their participation in the walk. Prizes will be awarded based on fundraising levels. All walkers who raise at least $80 will receive a T-shirt. Family Counseling Connection provides individual, family and group counseling by experienced, master's-level therapists. If you or someone you know has been sexually assaulted, confidential help is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week at 304-340-3676.

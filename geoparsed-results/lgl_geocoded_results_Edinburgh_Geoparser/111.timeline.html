BREAKING NEWS: Interstate 64 lanes reopen after accident. CHARLESTON, W.Va. -- All lanes of Interstate 64 East at Cross Lanes were closed because of an automobile accident, according to Metro 911. By 11:35 a.m., dispatchers were reporting that all the lanes had reopened. An earlier accident shut down the fast lane of Interstate 64 West at South Charlston. The lane was reopened by 10:17 a.m.

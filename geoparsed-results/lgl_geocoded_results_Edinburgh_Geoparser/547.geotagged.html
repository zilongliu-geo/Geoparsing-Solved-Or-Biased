Fire damages S.E. Paris apartment. Paris firefighters made quick work of a kitchen fire at the Spanish Oaks Apartments complex at 3125 Clarksville St. shortly after 10 p.m. Tuesday. The blaze was contained to the kitchen of one apartment, but heavy smoke was reported throughout the complex. Fire Chief Ronnie Grooms said 14 firemen responded to the scene, along with four EMS personnel and four Paris Policemen. No injuries were reported. It took firemen less than 30 minutes to extinguish the flames.

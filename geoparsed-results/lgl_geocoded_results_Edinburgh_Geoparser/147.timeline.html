BGA eighth-grade French students celebrate Mardi Gras. Eighth-grade French students at Battle Ground Academy recently celebrated Mardi Gras. Each year, the students lead an assembly for the middle school. A king and queen is selected randomly from each grade as well as a king and queen from the faculty. The tradition of throwing beads to the crowd is also carried out by the 8th graders.

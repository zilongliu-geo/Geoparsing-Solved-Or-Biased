Accident claims city mans life. RICHLAND ” A one-vehicle accident occurring about 4 a.m. Sunday morning claimed the life of Robin Garcia, 32, of Columbus. According to a report from the Colfax County Sheriff’s Department, Garcia was eastbound, driving a 2003 pickup 1/2 mile west of Richland on Highway 30 when the vehicle left the road, overturned and rolled several times. There were no passengers with Garcia. The pickup was a total loss.

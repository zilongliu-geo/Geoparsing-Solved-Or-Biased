Greenfield (formerly, Delkern) is a census-designated place in Kern County, California. It is located 7 miles (11 km) south of Bakersfield, at an elevation of 351 feet (107 m). The population was 3,991 at the 2010 census. The Delkern post office was opened in 1949. That name derives from Kern Delta, a designation for the surrounding area.

Grapeland center fund-raiser Friday. The annual fund-raiser meal and cake/pie auction will be held Friday at the Grapeland Senior Citizens Center. The “Souper Supper” serving will begin at 6 p.m. and will be followed by a short program of music and comedy by local talent. Cakes and pies will be auctioned by Virginia Chapin. The meal is free to the public. Donations may be made during the evening. Funds will go toward maintenance and refurbishing the center as well as expenses. For more information call Frances at 936-687-2849.

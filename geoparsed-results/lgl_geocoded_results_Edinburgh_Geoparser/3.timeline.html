Alexandria parents charged with hitting their child during fight between the two of them. rrested were Darryl Keith Warden, 44, of 2517 Memphis St., Alexandria, and Sheila D. Williams, 41, of 420 Augusta St., Alexandria. Both were charged with disturbing the peace by fighting and cruelty to a juvenile. Around 7:35 p.m. Sunday Alexandria Police went to Warden’s Memphis Street home where Williams said she’d gone to pick up her daughter. She told officers that when she arrived Warden, her ex-boyfriend, started a fight with her and hit her in the face. Warden told officers that Williams started the fight and that he didn’t hit her, according to the report.

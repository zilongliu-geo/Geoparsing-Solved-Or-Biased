Portland is a city in and the county seat of Jay County, Indiana, United States. The population was 6,223 at the 2010 census. Portland was platted in 1837. It was named after Portland, Maine. Portland is located at 40°26′2″N 84°58′48″W (40.433884, -84.979914), along the Salamonie River. According to the 2010 census, Portland has a total area of 4.653 square miles (12.05 km2), of which 4.65 square miles (12.04 km2) (or 99.94%) is land and 0.003 square miles (0.01 km2) (or 0.06%) is water.

Two counties rank as fastest growing . Two Northeast Georgia counties remain among the fastest-growing counties in the country, though their growth rates are slowing, new U.S. Census Bureau numbers reveal. Barrow County, previously ranked as the 12th fastest-growing U.S. county, fell to 18th in population growth. Jackson County ranked as the 25th fastest-growing county, down from 10th, according to Thursday's numbers. The 2009 ranking is based on population increases between July 1, 2007, and July 1, 2008.

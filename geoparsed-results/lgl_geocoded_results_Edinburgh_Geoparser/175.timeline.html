Docdate: 2022-01-17

Corps will reopen Bolding Mill campground. The park, located on the Chestatee River arm of Lake Lanier in North Hall, will open for the season on April 22 and close on Sept. 13. Bolding Mill features 53 campsites, some for tents and some with water and electricity hookups for RVs. The campground also has shower facilities, a playground and boat ramp. Reservations for campsites can be made through Reserve America, 877-345-6777.

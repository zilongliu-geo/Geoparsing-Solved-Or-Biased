Malaga (formerly, Tokay) is a census-designated place in Fresno County, California. It is located 6 miles (9.7 km) south-southeast of downtown Fresno, at an elevation of 295 feet (90 m). As of the 2010 census, Malaga had a population of 947. The post office, originally named Tokay, opened in 1886 and was renamed Malaga later that year.

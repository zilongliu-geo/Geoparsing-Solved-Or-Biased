Three arrested after mobile meth lab seized. Two men and a woman were arrested Tuesday after Lexington County sheriff’s deputies seized a mobile meth lab near Batesburg-Leesville, authorities said. Kevin G. Fulmer, 31, of Swansea, and Donald L. Schuler of Batesburg-Leesville and Crystal N. Bright of Lexington, both 28, are charged with manufacturing meth, a Lexington County Sheriff’s Department news release said. The lab was found in a sport utility vehicle on Rye Court after the car’s OnStar panic alarm system was activated, the release said.

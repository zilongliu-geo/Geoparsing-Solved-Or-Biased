Bella Vista is a census-designated place (CDP) in Shasta County, California. Bella Vista is a small, rural community about 9 miles (14 km) north-east of Redding, and about 5 miles (8.0 km) north of Palo Cedro. Bella Vista sits at an elevation of 656 feet (200 m). The 2010 United States census reported Bella Vista's population was 2,781. The 2010 United States Census reported that Bella Vista had a population of 2,781.

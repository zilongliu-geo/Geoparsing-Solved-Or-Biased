Woman found dead in Belgrade, Minn., house fire. BELGRADE, Minn. -- A woman was found dead by firefighters after they put out a blaze at a house in Belgrade in central Minnesota Saturday. The Stearns County sheriff's office said the house was filled with smoke when firefighters arrived at the scene. Once the fire was extinguished, they found the body of a 64-year-old woman inside. Her name was withheld pending notification of next of kin.

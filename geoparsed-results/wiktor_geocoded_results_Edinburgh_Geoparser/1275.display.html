Avondale is a borough in Chester County, Pennsylvania, United States. The population was 1,265 at the 2010 census. Avondale is located at 39°49′32″N 75°46′58″W (39.825609, -75.782763). The borough has a total area of 0.5 square miles (1.3 km2), all land. At the 2010 census, the borough was 30.7% non-Hispanic White, 8.9% Black or African American, 1.4% Native American, 0.8% Asian, and 2.8% were two or more races. 59.0% of the population were of Hispanic or Latino ancestry. As of the census of 2000, there are 1,108 people, 345 households, and 247 families residing in Avondale. The population density is 2,248.7 per square mile (873.1/km2). There are 361 housing units at an average density of 732.7 per square mile (284.5/km2). The racial makeup of Avondale is 69.77% White, 16.61% African American, 0.90% Native American, 0.27% Asian, 10.38% from other races, and 2.08% from two or more races. Hispanic or Latino of any race account for 38.09% of the population.

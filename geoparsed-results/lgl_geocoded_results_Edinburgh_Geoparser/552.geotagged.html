Gymnastics: Hagerstown advances to state meet. Hagerstown advanced to the state meet for the first time as a team after finishing second during the IHSAA regional Friday at Columbus East. Columbus North won the event with 111.05 points, the Tigers were next with 109.925, while New Castle was third with 106.55. The top-two teams at the regional advanced to the March 21 state meet at Perry Meridian.

Businessmen plead in money laundering case. Los Angeles Superior Court Judge David Horwitz sentenced each man to two years probation and ordered each to pay a total of $102,600 in fines and penalties. As a condition of their probation, they are forbidden from contributing to any Los Angeles City political campaign for the duration of their probation.

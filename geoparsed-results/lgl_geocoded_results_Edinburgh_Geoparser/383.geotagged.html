Parenting conference set at Story. Palestine Independent School District Parent Involvement Coordinator Craig Harris will present a parenting conference for parents of Story Elementary School students at 6:30 p.m. March 26 in the school library. Harris will talk about parenting issues like being consistent, helping children make good decisions, discipline versus punishment, and helping children think with their upper brain functions. The conference is free to all parents of Story School students in the district. Snacks will be provided and there will be a special presentation by the Story Elementary School Choir.

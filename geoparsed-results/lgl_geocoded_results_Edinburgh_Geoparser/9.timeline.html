Drivers safety course offered in Parkers Prairie. April 14: four-hour refresher course from 6 to 10 p.m. at Parkers Prairie High School, Room 113, Highway 29, Parkers Prairie. For more information or to register, call toll free 1-888-234-1294. The Driver Improvement course is open to the public; however, pre-registration is requested. A Minnesota Highway Safety and Research Center certified instructor teaches this class. Driver safety, confidence and comfort are enhanced by utilizing the most up-to-date research in the field, including the latest visual scanning and steering techniques, anti-lock braking (ABS) skills, airbag information, driver distraction, road rage and new vehicle technology.

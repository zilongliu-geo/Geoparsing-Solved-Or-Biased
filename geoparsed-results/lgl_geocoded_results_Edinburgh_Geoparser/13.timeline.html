Docdate: 2022-01-17

Driver injured in Nokomis crash. An Alexandria man remains hospitalized after a two-vehicle crash at the intersection of North Nokomis Street and Darling Avenue near the Re/Max realty office in Alexandria Friday night. Steven Sachs of Alexandria was driving a Mazda and James Tauber of Nelson was driving a Dodge Caravan. Sachs, 58, was taken to the Douglas County Hospital and later transferred to St. Cloud Hospital where he was listed in fair condition Tuesday morning. No other injuries were reported. The crash happened just before 9 p.m.

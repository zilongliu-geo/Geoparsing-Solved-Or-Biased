El Rancho is a census-designated place (CDP) in Tulare County, California. El Rancho sits at an elevation of 390 feet (120 m). The 2010 United States census reported El Rancho's population was 124. The 2010 United States Census reported that El Rancho had a population of 124. The population density was 1,722.0 people per square mile (664.9/km²). The racial makeup of El Rancho was 71 (57.3%) White, 1 (0.8%) African American, 1 (0.8%) Native American, 0 (0.0%) Asian, 0 (0.0%) Pacific Islander, 49 (39.5%) from other races, and 2 (1.6%) from two or more races. Hispanic or Latino of any race were 117 persons (94.4%). The Census reported that 124 people (100% of the population) lived in households, 0 (0%) lived in non-institutionalized group quarters, and 0 (0%) were institutionalized. There were 29 households, out of which 12 (41.4%) had children under the age of 18 living in them, 17 (58.6%) were opposite-sex married couples living together, 7 (24.1%) had a female householder with no husband present, 2 (6.9%) had a male householder with no wife present. There were 1 (3.4%) unmarried opposite-sex partnerships, and 0 (0%) same-sex married couples or partnerships. 3 households (10.3%) were made up of individuals and 1 (3.4%) had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 4.28. There were 26 families (89.7% of all households); the average family size was 4.35.

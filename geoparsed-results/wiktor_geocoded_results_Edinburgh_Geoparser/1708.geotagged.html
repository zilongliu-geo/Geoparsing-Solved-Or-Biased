Marlborough is a residential neighbourhood in the north-east quadrant of Calgary, Alberta. It is bounded by the Trans-Canada Highway to the north, 52 Street E to the east, Memorial Drive to the south and 36 Street E to the west. The community is served by the Marlborough station of the C-Train LRT system, and Marlborough Mall is located in the south-west corner of the neighbourhood. Marlborough was established in 1967, and was named after Marlborough, England. It is represented in the Calgary City Council by the Ward 10 councillor. The postal code in this area is T2A.

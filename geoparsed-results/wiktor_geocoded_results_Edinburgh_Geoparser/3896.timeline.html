Norwood is a town in Stanly County, North Carolina, United States. The population was 2,379 at the 2010 census. Norwood is located at 35°13′32″N 80°7′12″W (35.225574, -80.120042). According to the United States Census Bureau, the town has a total area of 2.4 square miles (6.2 km2), all of it land. U.S. Highway 52 passes through the town between Wadesboro and Albemarle. Norwood is currently served by two rail roads and one US Highway and one NC Route. Water and sewer service is provided by the town with their own water and sewer plant. A County branch library and museum provides education opportunities to the town. Bordered by Lake Tillery and the Rocky River, recreation is abundant and plentiful. Golf is available at Piney Point Golf Club. Fork Stables is located about 2 miles south of town and the Michelin tire plant which makes the aircraft tires for the space shuttle is located in town. Police and Fire protection is provided by the town by full-time officers. Norwood is nestled in a beautiful area between two rivers next to the Uwharrie Mountains. Besides the multitude of recreation and entertainment opportunities nearby there are also local places for visitors and residents to enjoy.

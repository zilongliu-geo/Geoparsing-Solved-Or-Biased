Ex-minister meets with Hampton pupils to talk about their Rwanda website. A former Government minister met with students to discuss a website they have created. The website was created by students who have been studying the Rwanda genocide and is about remembering the 1994 events and explaining why it is important to do so. Mr Mitchell was accompanied by Deborah Thomas, Conservative candidate for the Twickenham parliamentary seat, who said she was delighted to be involved.

Rochester man says home was burglarized while he slept . A Rochester man was sleeping when burglars entered his home and stole a computer and other electronics Sunday morning, according to police. Police Capt. Brian Winters said the burglary was reported at 9:47 a.m. The 26-year-old victim said he went to sleep about 4 a.m. Sunday and woke up about 9:30 a.m. to find the items missing. Entry was believed to be gained through an unlocked door. The home is located in the 900 block of 14th Avenue Northeast.

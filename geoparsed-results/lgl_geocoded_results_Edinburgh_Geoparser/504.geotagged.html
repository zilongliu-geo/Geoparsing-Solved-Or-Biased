Four competing to be mayor of Ozark. Watts, 62, spent 40 years working for Fasco Industries. He has been mayor of Ozark twice -- from 1990 to 1996 and from 2007 to the present. He also served as an alderman for 16 years. He is a past president of the Ozark Chamber of Commerce and currently sits on the board. He spent six years in the Missouri National Guard while attending Draughn Business College in Springfield.
